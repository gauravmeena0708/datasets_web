Methodologies & Performance

Comparison of key synthetic data generation techniques found in the literature.

TabDDPM (2022)

Core: Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model.

  • Encoding: Gaussian Quantile for numerical, One-hot for categorical.
  • Process: Adds Gaussian noise to embeddings, learns to reverse it.
  • Pros: SOTA performance on many tasks, handles mixed types well.

TabSyn (2024)

Core: Latent Diffusion Model (VAE + Diffusion).

  • Strategy: Compresses data into a latent space using a VAE first.
  • Benefit: Faster sampling than TabDDPM (diffusion happens in smaller latent space).
  • Key Result: Better structure preservation (shapes) than pure diffusion.

GReaT (2022)

Core: LLM Fine-tuning (DistilGPT-2).

  • Technique: Serializes tabular rows into text sentences ("Age is 30, Income is...").
  • Pros: Excellent semantic understanding, conditioning is natural language.
  • Cons: Slow training/sampling, context window limits.

Performance Comparison (Adult Dataset)

Machine Learning Efficacy (F1-Score / Accuracy). Higher is better.

Note: Real data baseline is usually ~0.85-0.86 for Adult. TabDDPM and TabSyn consistently reach >0.84, often outperforming GAN-based methods (CTGAN ~0.81).

Evaluation Metrics

Common formulas used to validate synthetic data quality.

Machine Learning Efficacy (MLE)

Utility

Also known as TSTR (Train on Synthetic, Test on Real).

Score = Accuracy(Msynth on Dreal)

Interpretation: If the score is close to "Train on Real, Test on Real", the synthetic data successfully captured the predictive signals.

Wasserstein Distance

Fidelity

Measures the distance between the distribution of real and synthetic columns.

W(P, Q) = inf E[d(x, y)]

Interpretation: Lower is better. 0 means distributions are identical. Usually calculated column-wise and averaged.

Distance to Closest Record (DCR)

Privacy

Ensures synthetic data is not just copying real data.

DCR = min || xsynth - xreal ||

Interpretation: We want this distance to be non-zero (to avoid memorization) but not too large (to maintain realism).

Correlation Matrix Difference

Structure

Checks if pairwise relationships are preserved.

|| Corr(Real) - Corr(Synth) ||2

Interpretation: Lower is better. Visualized often as a heatmap difference.
